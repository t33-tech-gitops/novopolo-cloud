NOVOPOLO Cloud
Property & Operations Management

SaaS for real estate & facility operations. Hierarchical portfolio (condominium → block → unit), document lifecycle & digital signatures, service orders, inter‑department workflows, and executive dashboards — all powered by Firebase, AppSheet and Google Workspace.

Talk to Sales See architecture Google products

Core capabilities

  • Portfolio model: Condominium → Building/Block → Unit with ownership rotation.
  • Document control: contracts, permits, service orders, expirations, versioning and retention (5–10 years).
  • Digital signing: Gov.br compatible (via external provider) and audit trail.
  • Operational flows between departments (HR → Finance, Facilities → Rentals, etc.).
  • Notifications: Gmail templates & Google Chat Webhooks by business area.

Data & security

  • RBAC with Firebase Authentication & Custom Claims.
  • Firestore rules (least privilege) & Cloud Functions validators.
  • LGPD‑aligned: purpose limitation, access logs, retention & data subject requests.
  • Backups & vault policy for user data and documents.

Analytics & governance

  • Operational KPIs in Looker Studio (Data Studio).
  • Executive exports: HTML, Canva/PPTX packs for board reports.
  • SLA monitoring for expiring documents and service orders.

Google products used

Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Functions, Hosting) AppSheet (automations & apps) Google Workspace (Gmail, Drive, Chat, Sheets, Docs) Looker Studio Cloud Storage Cloud Run (optional microservices)
